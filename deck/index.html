The Foundation

Co-living

Our everyday spaces give slowmads, cultural explorers, and independent creators a calm, reliable place to anchor their lives.

  • Travelers, couples, and remote workers are actively seeking genuine community over chaotic hostels or isolating corporate apartments.
  • By providing a beautiful, distraction-free home that caters to rest-seekers and explorers alike, we build deep trust with our long-term residents.
  • This constant, organic presence from our core audience gives our properties a stable, dependable heartbeat all year round.

For Everyone

Hotaru Retreats

We handle the quiet background work so passionate hosts can create meaningful gatherings for all kinds of travelers.

  • Our retreats are not just for companies; they are designed for all our guests—from solo rest-seekers and couples to groups of friends and small teams.
  • We internally manage all the retreat planning, costing, and logistics so the experience feels effortless for whoever joins us.
  • Our gatherings are host-led—we carefully find the right guides and authentic on-ground partners to lead our guests under the Hotaru Retreats banner.

Our White-Label Offering

The Larisa Partnership

We quietly power in-house retreats for our sister brands.

  • We bring the Hotaru standard of care and organization to their spaces without overshadowing their unique identities.
